What it is

Panda is a game for the hustlers, guiding start-ups to getting their businesses started and running in a competitive leaderboard. Connecting entrepreneurs, researchers and investors in a single, interactive, classifieds.

How it works

There are three key players:

1. Entrepreneurs 2. Investors 3. Researchers

Entrepreneurs register with their CIPC Customer Code, and are guided into starting up their business through objectives and goals to achieve.

As they achieve these goals and grow, they earn points and experience , which can be spent on tangible benefits for their company and business, e.g. A sit down with an Investor , advertising advantages, services, etc. Entrepreneurs are limited to one company in the game, with a limited lifespan in the game.

Investors, as their name suggests, can Invest in the Entrepreneurs and give them advantages in the game.

Researchers analyse the game and its players, giving insight and analytics. They can also offer their services to the Entrepreneurs
